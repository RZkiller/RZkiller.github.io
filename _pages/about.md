## Research and Project Experience

My recent work focuses on **cross-view visual understanding** and its applications to **embodied perception**.

For example, in **ObjectRelator**, I developed a multimodal framework for understanding object relations across egocentric and exocentric views, improving robustness under large viewpoint shifts and complex backgrounds. In **V²-SAM**, I worked on equipping SAM2 with cross-view correspondence ability through complementary geometric and appearance-based prompting mechanisms.

Beyond academic research, I have also worked on robotics systems and real-world deployment, including:

- a **mobile manipulation robot for autonomous coffee delivery**, integrating mapping, navigation, calibration, communication, and 6D pose estimation;
- an **optical motion capture-based robotic arm error compensation system** for industrial welding scenarios;
- research internships related to **automatic welding** and **robotic grasping**, including work on sim-to-real transfer and IL+RL-based policy design.
